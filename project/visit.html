Dog Training Options

Basic Obedience Package

$199

Perfect for: First-time dog owners or basic behavior needs. This package includes: 3 private training sessions, basic commands (sit, come, down, stay), customized at-home training plan, and email support for 1 week. Results: Lay a solid foundation for obedience and communication.

Advanced Training Package

$299

Perfect for: Dogs needing stronger obedience or moderate behavior correction. Includes everything in Basic, PLUS: 5 private training sessions (1 hour each), advanced commands (Leave it, Heel, Place, Wait, Distraction and Real-World Environment training), problem behavior correction (e.g., jumping, barking), follow-up progress check-in, and phone/email support for 2 weeks. Results: Improved responsiveness, impulse control, and confidence in various situations.

Elite Behavior & Lifestyle Package

$399

Perfect for: Long-term results, behavior changes, or prepping for therapy/service work. Includes everything in Advanced, PLUS: 7 private training sessions (1 hour each), customized training for specific needs (e.g., leash reactivity, anxiety), ongoing review of at-home training, priority scheduling & lifetime email support, graduation certificate, and training tools like a treat pouch and clicker. Results: A well-rounded, confident dog with long-lasting behavioral improvements.
